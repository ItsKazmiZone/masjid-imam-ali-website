Our Sanctuary

The Heart of Our Community Center

We offer multiple specialized facilities designed to nurture spiritual tranquility, academic research, youth development, and historic remembrance.

Main Prayer Hall

A beautifully designed, light-filled, carpeted prayer hall featuring high arched ceilings and geometric windows. Built specifically to accommodate daily congregational prayers, quiet meditation, and soulful supplications in a high-contrast and acoustically optimized atmosphere.

Imambargah

Our dedicated hall for religious assemblies, grief mourning, and spiritual lectures. A serene sanctuary designed for Majalis, Wiladat celebrations, and community programs honoring the purified progeny of the Holy Prophet (a.s.).

Husayniyah

Islamic Library & Research

Housing a treasure trove of books, classical manuscripts, and modern electronic research tools. Our research library specializes in Quranic exegesis, Shia jurisprudence, Islamic history, and the authentic books of Hadith (Kutub al-Arba'ah).
