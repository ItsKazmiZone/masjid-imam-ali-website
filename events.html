Commemoration

Annual Ashura Majalis Assembly

10 Muharram 1448 AH
7:30 PM - 10:30 PM

Daily from 1st to 10th Muharram

English lecture & Latmiyah mourning prayers

7:00 PM - 8:45 PM

6:30 PM - 9:30 PM

Includes festive community dinner
