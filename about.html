Who We Are

Our History, Mission & Legacy

Serving the community with deep devotion, guided by the timeless legacy of Ameer al-Mu'mineen Imam Ali ibn Abi Talib (a.s.).

Our Purpose

The Divine Core Mission

Our mission is to establish a welcoming spiritual and academic home where individuals, families, and youth can deepen their understanding of Islam, refine their characters, and serve humanity selflessly.

We seek to foster interfaith collaboration, absolute scholarly research, moral growth, and sincere brotherhood. Guided by the values of righteousness, patience, and supreme justice as demonstrated by Imam Ali (a.s.).

Compassion

Providing absolute welfare and food assistance to community members in need.

Knowledge

Offering rich Quranic interpretation, Islamic history, and jurisprudence lessons.

A Century of Faith

The Sacred Legacy

Established over several decades, the Masjid Imam Ali center has transitioned from a small rental prayer hall into an acclaimed masterpiece of modern Islamic architecture and dynamic academic dialogue.

1988

Humble Beginnings

A small group of devout scholars and community pioneers rent a simple storefront to establish daily prayers and a weekend school for youth.

2002

Purchasing Our Land

With generous donations from community members, the present-day land was purchased to construct a dedicated, custom-designed community center.

2018

Grand Opening of the Masjid

Construction of our beautiful emerald-gold styled sanctuary, library, and grand prayer hall is fully completed and officially inaugurated by prominent international scholars.
